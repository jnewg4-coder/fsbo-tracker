AI Deal Advisor

The Advisor is the fastest way to turn the raw listing feed into action. It does not create data; it helps you work with the data already in your account more efficiently.

The problem

Without an advisor layer, users still bounce between calculators, alerts, and note fragments. The add-on matters because it cuts the orchestration overhead, not because it replaces judgment.

What it does

  • Run quick deal math such as ARV scenarios, 70% rule checks, cap-rate style reasoning, and offer framing.
  • Explain why a listing scored the way it did using the current scoring breakdown.
  • Create or edit saved searches with natural language instead of form-only setup.
  • Answer market questions using the markets and listings already available in the product.

Current shape

  • Available as a $10/month add-on on paid plans.
  • Includes 50 messages per month with optional top-ups.
  • Three voice modes are available: Direct Pro, Patient Mentor, and Deal Hawk.

Best use

  • Shortening the path from “interesting listing” to “worth an offer?”
  • Turning filters and saved-search setup into conversation.
  • Supporting newer team members without forcing them to learn your whole acquisition playbook first.

Example workflow

  • Ask the Advisor to surface live listings matching your buy box instead of rebuilding filters from scratch.
  • Use it to frame next-step math: “is this cosmetic or gut?” “what would a conservative offer range look like?”
  • Create or tighten a saved search from the conversation once you know what you want repeated daily.

Typical workflow

01

Screen

Use score, photos, price movement, and filters to narrow the field quickly.

02

Investigate

Open only the shortlisted listings for contact, notes, Advisor prompts, and deeper math.

03

Decide

Move finalists into real comps, local due diligence, and your actual offer process.
