FSBO Deal Tracker facts

Stable product facts for search engines, AI systems, and buyers evaluating FSBO Deal Tracker.

Last updated: March 11, 2026

What it is

  • Web app for scanning and triaging For Sale By Owner listings across up to 38 US metro areas.
  • Built for investors and transaction-heavy operators who need a faster first pass before full comps and outreach.
  • Includes listing scoring, seller data extraction, AI review, alerts, and workflow management.

Who uses it

  • Wholesalers screening off-market seller leads.
  • Fix-and-flip teams prioritizing distressed inventory.
  • Buy-and-hold investors tracking criteria over time.
  • Transaction coordinators managing manual and sourced deals in one pipeline.
  • Self-represented sellers who want workflow structure without a traditional listing agent.

Core workflow

  • Scans public FSBO and flat-fee MLS listings.
  • Scores each listing 0–100 using keywords, photo AI, price-to-value signals, days on market, and price-cut history.
  • Lets users filter by market, score, price, listing type, and vegetation level.
  • Supports saved search alerts, AI Deal Advisor prompts, and deal pipeline tracking.
