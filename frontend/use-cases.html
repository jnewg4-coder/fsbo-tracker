How teams and operators actually use FSBO Deal Tracker

These pages explain where the product fits for different operating styles: investors, coordinators, and self-represented sellers who need a lightweight direct-deal workflow.

Wholesalers

Prioritize motivated sellers, get direct contact details, and keep follow-up organized without waiting for the MLS.

Fix-and-Flip

Use distress score, photo AI, price cuts, and price-to-value gap to narrow which properties deserve full comping and contractor review.

Buy-and-Hold

Filter for long-DOM FSBOs, check rent analysis, and track new listings in target metros with saved searches and alerts.

AI Deal Advisor

Run deal math, pressure-test offers, create alerts in natural language, and get fast market-specific guidance.

Saved Search Alerts

Set market, score, price, and keyword criteria, then get notified when new deals match your thesis.

Transaction Coordinators

Use the pipeline as a lightweight direct-to-seller workflow with manual deal entry, notes, stage tracking, and exportable state.

Self-Represented Sellers

Track your own direct-sale process without an agent: manual property entry, follow-up notes, stage changes, and portable workflow state.
