FAQ

How does billing work?
Subscriptions are billed monthly via Helcim (secure card processing). Your card is tokenized — we never see your full card number. You can cancel anytime from your account settings.
Can I switch plans?
Yes. Upgrade instantly from your account. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
What markets are available?
Up to 28 metro areas including Charlotte, Nashville, Tampa, Atlanta, Philadelphia, Jacksonville, Memphis, Houston, Dallas, Detroit, Orlando, Raleigh, Pittsburgh, St. Louis, Kansas City, and more. New markets added based on demand.
What happens when I cancel?
Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period. After that, you're downgraded to the free tier. No data is deleted.
